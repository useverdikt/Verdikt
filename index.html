How it works

When you're ready to ship a release,
intelligence and certification are right there.

Your eval pipeline runs as normal. Verdikt sits in your release flow: suggestions to tune thresholds, clear verdicts at ship time, and a permanent accountability record for overrides.

01
You define the standard
Set thresholds per signal category. Verdikt suggests where to tighten or raise based on release history. You decide what to apply.
THRESHOLDS
02
Verdikt issues the verdict
Every release is evaluated against your thresholds. If all signals pass — CERTIFIED. If thresholds fail — UNCERTIFIED. One status, visible to everyone.
VERDICT
03
Overrides require named accountability
If a team chooses to ship below threshold: a named owner is required, structured justification is mandatory, and the record is permanent.
OVERRIDE
